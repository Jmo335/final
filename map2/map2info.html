This is a contour map that is hosted by MapBox and is concentrated around my area of interest.

The map showcases contour lines that can be interpreted to gauge where to cut and fill dirt.

This can help make a decision on where to remove dirt and also help determine where to place it.

The idea of constructing a road to help ascend this hill will indeed be a task, the idea of visualizing these slopes with the presence of contours is really helpful.
